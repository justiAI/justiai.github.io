Product background

Access to justice is a systems problem

Access to justice remains a fundamental challenge in Taiwan, particularly for migrant workers and low-income individuals. Traditional legal-tech focuses on advice — but users need procedural clarity. JustiAI bridges this gap by providing step-by-step navigation through existing legal institutions, reducing barriers without regulatory risks.

Core value

Simplifying what feels
impossibly complex

JustiAI focuses on simplifying legal procedures into clear, structured, and user-friendly steps.

01

Step-by-step guidance

Breaks down complex legal procedures into simple actions, documents, and next steps anyone can follow.

02

Navigation, not advice

Provides procedural direction while avoiding legal advice, keeping compliance risk minimal for both users and platform.

03

Inclusive design

Supports multilingual access and user-friendly experiences designed specifically for underserved communities.
